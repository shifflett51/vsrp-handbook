Staff Handbook

Virginia State Roleplay

This staff handbook will go over all the most important elements of being a staff member at Virginia State Roleplay. For all staff members, but new staff members especially, it's highly suggested you read over this and know its contents.

1 Staff Rules

  • ELS is allowed on duty but no siren.
  • Always use grammar on-duty.
  • Never use a gun on-duty.
  • Never park in the middle of the road when responding to a call.
  • Always use 4+ letter commands.
  • Do not RP while on-duty, except when using 50/50.
  • Always follow traffic laws while on-duty.
  • Never use commands off-duty.
  • Only one moderator per call, unless they request help.
  • Only take video evidence and/or 3+ witnesses as proof.
  • Do not use :h and :m messages for unnecessary reasons.
  • If you are the last moderator on, and are getting off you must notify HR.
  • You are not allowed to engage in pursuits as staff.
  • We do NOT do STS
  • DO NOT USE ALL COMMANDS

3 Melonly

Before you can begin moderating, you must go to your Melonly moderator panel and start your shift. When you warn/kick/ban someone you must log it in the moderator panel by entering their username in the middle and select the punishment type and reason.

4 Infractions

As a member of the staff team you can be warned, striked, suspended or terminated. A warning is the most common and least serious infraction. 3 warnings are equal to one strike. Warnings expire after 2 weeks and strikes expire after 3 weeks. It is your job to make a management ticket to have them removed when the time has passed.
